Research

The Computational Social Science (CSS) group focuses on a span of research problems which are of concerns to society. The problems include misinformation, hate speech, mental health, segregation in the society, Bias and Fairness in AI models. The group analyses digital traces such as mobile call data records, financial transactions and most importantly social media web to investigate the problems.
